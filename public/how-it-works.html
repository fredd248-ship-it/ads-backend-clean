How It Works

Purpose
ADS helps you track decisions, evaluate real outcomes, and identify patterns in how you make choices over time.
How Decisions Work
1. Create a decision
2. Use or experience the outcome
3. Evaluate it later
4. Let the system detect patterns
Evaluation
Each decision is evaluated based on satisfaction, frequency of use, and whether you would make the same decision again.
Insights
The system analyzes your decisions over time to highlight patterns such as consistency, impulse risk, and overall decision quality.
Categories
Decisions are grouped into categories to help you understand patterns across different areas of your life.
